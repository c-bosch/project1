About Christine

Christine is an illustration student at the Sam Fox school of Art and Design at Washington University in St. Louis.
She is an enthusiast of many forms of art from scupture to photography to that one scarf she tried to knit that one time and almost finished.

More successful projects of hers inclue a hand drawn animation, some wedding invitations, and a recycled mural that is likely still in her parents' basement to this day. Sorry Mom and Dad.

But this site is not about those things.
